          Question on streaming (OBS)

          in Other

          Posted September 19, 2018

          You will need a capture device for your PC. It can be external (such as via USB 3.0) or internal (such as a capture card you install directly into your PC).

          My setup is as follows:

          -PS4 uses HDMI out
          -HDMI out cord goes into an HDMI splitter
          -One output from the HDMI splitter goes to my TV
          -The second output from my HDMI splitter goes to my external capture device, which connects to my PC via USB 3.0
          -OBS detects the external capture device as a video feed with audio, and allows me to capture/stream it

          Pretty simple. Then of course, you'd need a mic and webcam for the PC as well.
