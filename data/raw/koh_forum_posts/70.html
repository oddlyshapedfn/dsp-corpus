          Other uploaders don't have this issue because they 1. upload far less than me (so it doesn't require you to sit in front of the PC all that time) or 2. schedule out their uploads far in advance, so the thumbnails have time to show up.           Can I respond to the "racist humor" stuff? Because I do see people bring it up often and I'd like to address it.

          I am not racist nor do I judge anyone based on physical appearance, mannerism or sex CONSCIOUSLY (there will always be subconscious things you can't really help). I've always called myself and equal-opportunity jokester and you will hear me make fun of everyone - Asians, Rednecks, Middle-Easterners, Indians, Native Americans, British, French, Italians, Europeans, Mexicans etc. - in a joking manner. I never mean it and, being a part of the Street Fighter community for so long, I had TONS of friends/rivals from all races, creeds and sexual orientations. I never judged someone by that.

          There will always be people who find this kind of humor offensive. Just like there will always be people who find ANYTHING touching the subjects of religion, politics, etc. offensive. Those people will never be swayed one way or the other, and that's okay. The bottom line is, I'm FAR from a G-rated person and I will make funny jokes/observations/impressions if I find it humorous at the time. Some may say that some go over the line (when I impersonated a Nazi character from the Howard Stern show some FOUR years ago now, for example), but for the MOST part I'd like to think that I do it in a fair manner that isn't meant to offend.

          It would be one thing if you could bring up a running pattern of me making life judgments and incredibly unfair/racist tirades that form a history/pattern of race discrimination, but for the life of me in the 6 years I've been doing this on YouTube, I can't think of ONE time I actually did something in a non-joking manner based on race. So...I don't understand how anyone, unless they were trying to slander me, could say I was "racist" without any kind of concrete proof.

          My advice to you is, if you don't like racial-style humor, I'm probably not for you. It won't come up ALL the time but it WILL come up, and you WILL probably be offended if it bothers you. But the vast majority of people who've watched my content have never said I was racist nor accused me of it, because they know it's all in jest.

          That's my two cents, anyway.
