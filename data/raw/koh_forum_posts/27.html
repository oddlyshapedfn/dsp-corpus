          Disappointed to Hear that Phil is not playing Season 2 of SF5

          in General

          Posted December 23, 2016

          I don't think it's a "hate bandwagon" at all, actually, It's just the first time since the release of SF4 that Street Fighter is no longer "popular" outside of the hardcore fighting game community. Capcom really has no idea how badly they've tarnished their image over the years, from dumbing down their huge franchises (RE, DMC) to nickel-and-diming the customer for DLC that should have been in the game for free (Street Fighter x Tekken, Asura's Wrath, and recently Dead Rising 4).

          Releasing SFV in the shitty unfinished state it was sold for $60 at, it completely demolished the confidence of the mainstream consumer. YES, tons of people watch SFV on stream, but sadly they didn't buy the fucking game! They just watch it on stream and buy into the hype of tournaments - but that does NOT sell copies of games alone. 

          A successful fighting game has to do the following:

          -Provide something not already present in other fighters at the time
          -Prove to be competitively sound
          -Also have enough content to interest the more casual fans, who may watch streams and play the game, but never actually participate at a competitive level

           

          At launch, SFV did NONE of these. Today? It does one: the last, after adding a Story mode, character trials and the like FAR TOO LATE for anybody to care. 

          Sorry, SFV is still a shitty game for competitive play. It still has 6.5 milliseconds of input lag, THE MOST OF ANY COMPETITIVE FIGHTER ON THE MARKET, and is the least-reactionary fighting game in history. You cannot react to most things that happen, instead only playing patterns and "Safe" strategies where you "play the odds" that it'll work, since there's no actual way to stop it once it starts being effective due to the input lag. It's the ENTIRE REASON R.Mika is even viable in the damn game. Anyone who has played anything competitively will tell you: MORE LAG IS BAD!

          It's just finally come to the point where people who aren't into the competitive FGC don't give a fuck anymore, me included. This game will always be shit and they need to go back to the drawing board. I'm not going to support this broken piece of crap; I gave it MORE THAN ENOUGH TIME in 2016 already and I have no desire to spend more time, effort and money on a sub-par, rushed, incredibly poorly developed game by a hack dev team that didn't deserve to be handed the mantle of the mainstream SF series to begin with.

          The sooner people stop supporting crap, the sooner stuff will actually get better.
